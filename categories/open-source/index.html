MacVim is back on track

It’s no secret I’m a big fan of Vim. Although I can use it in the terminal, I like to have a nice UI to look at and when I’m on a Mac (e.g., at work), I use MacVim. I have no words to thank the amazing job behind both Vim and MacVim and I always wanted to contribute back. I’ve been building MacVim for older versions of Mac since 2012 and since Björn has been busy, I’m maintaining it now.
