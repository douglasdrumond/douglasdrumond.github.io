Hello World!

Posted by Douglas Drumond Kayama on February 23, 2010 · 1 min read

Edit: In 2010 I and Daniel Cariello, one of my best friends, set up a blog called Cafe Linear. This was our first post.

Searching for same happiness we had when talking about linear algebra (sometimes, computational math too) at IMECC’s cafe, drinking coffee (thank you Zefa), and, of course, standing Daniel’s wonderful jokes \( (N/m^{2}) \), we present this blog. Virtually we may experience same joy and share a little of our (lack of) knowledge in math and C.S.

A quote from Alice in Wonderland that represents we authors and readers ;) :

“Would you tell me, please, which way I ought to go from here?”
“That depends a good deal on where you want to get to,” said the Cat.
“I don’t much care where —” said Alice.
“Then it doesn’t matter which way you go,” said the Cat
“But I don’t want to go among mad people,” Alice remarked.
“Oh, you can’t help that,” said the Cat: “we’re all mad here. I’m mad. You’re mad.”
“How do you know I’m mad?” said Alice.
“You must be,” said the Cat, “or you wouldn’t have come here.”

We’re glad you’re here. Enjoy the madness.
